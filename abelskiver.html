Abelskiver

Description:

Aebelskiver, a delightful Danish treat, is a spherical pancake filled with various sweet or savory ingredients. This fluffy and indulgent pastry is a beloved part of Danish culinary tradition, often enjoyed for breakfast or as a festive treat.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Vanilla extract
  • Your grandma's hand-me-down abelskiver pan, guaranteed to create perfectly spherical delights

Preparation

  1. Prepare the batter by blending flour, sugar, eggs, melted butter, and a hint of vanilla extract until smooth.
  2. Heat a specialized abelskiver pan over a stovetop until hot.
  3. Grease the pan with butter or oil and pour batter into each well, filling them halfway.
  4. Add your desired filling, such as jam, Nutella, or cheese, to each well.
  5. Using a skewer or chopsticks, gently turn the abelskiver as they cook, ensuring even browning on all sides.
  6. Once golden brown and cooked through, remove the abelskiver from the pan and serve warm.
  7. Sprinkle with powdered sugar or serve with a dollop of whipped cream for an extra touch of indulgence.
